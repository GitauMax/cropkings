CROP PATHOLOGY

The most critical issue for profitable management of crop diseases is getting the correct diagnosis. We provide disease diagnostic services. Disease development in plants is influenced by many factors including, variety genetics of the crop, age of the plant, environment (Weather, Soil, Farm Operations etc.), single versus mixed infections, and the pathogen populations genetics. We provide information on the biology of pathogens, the environmental conditions that favor disease development and approaches to take to manage diseases. Once correct diagnosis has been done, we then advise you on how to handle the problem.
