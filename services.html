our services

AGRONOMY TRAININGS

Farmers need trainings to make informed growing decisions. At Grow and Glow Agribusiness, we have a team of Agronomists experts that will walk the crop with you and advice you on the best approaches for optimum yield and cost-effective production. They will share knowledge of agronomy techniques and skills that can improve your productivity

SCIENTIFIC TRIALS

We conduct field trials for seed varieties and agro-chemicals to determine the most suitable crop varieties and effective agro-chemicals for crop production. Monitoring and evaluation of the trials is keenly done, data collected and analyzed using GenStat or SAS and resulted presented. Additionally, we conduct field demonstrations. Our trial team is independent and as such only recommends products based on performance.

CROP PATHOLOGY

The most critical issue for profitable management of crop diseases is getting the correct diagnosis. We provide disease diagnostic services. Disease development in plants is influenced by many factors including, variety genetics of the crop, age of the plant, environment (Weather, Soil, Farm Operations etc.), single versus mixed infections, and the pathogen populations genetics.

CROP NUTRITION

We promote using the right fertilizer source, at the right rate, at the right time, and in the right place so as to increase yields and profitability while protecting the environment and improving sustainability. Crops thrive from receiving proper nutrition more efficiently. We formulate the best fertilizer and fertigation programs

CROP ENTERPRISE MANAGEMENT

At grow and glow agribusiness, we provide best solution to agribusiness entrepreneur. We offer professional advice on venture choice, cost-benefit analysis to business plan and overall farm management.
We provide advises in good farm management.

CROP PROTECTION

Our crop protection experts will help you keep your crop healthy and safe, free from pests and diseases using the IPM approach. Services that we offer here include:
1. Formulating the best spray programs
2. Pests and disease prevention and control
3. Calibration of spray systems and equipment
